CuisineEase — Simple Testing Guide

Who is this for? Anyone testing CuisineEase — no technical skills needed. You only need a phone or computer, a web browser, and an email address. Follow each step in order.

Important tips before you start
  • Use two browsers (or one normal window + one private/incognito window): one for the restaurant manager, one for the customer.
  • Write down every email and password you create.
  • Check your inbox and spam folder for emails from CuisineEase.
  • If something does not work, take a screenshot and note the exact step number.

What are you testing?

User typeWhat they do on CuisineEase
Restaurant managerRegisters a restaurant, adds food to the menu, manages orders, tables, and reservations
CustomerCreates an account, browses restaurants, orders food, pays, books tables, and leaves reviews
Visitor (no account)Browses the public website, reads about CuisineEase, and sends a contact message

Recommended test order (follow this sequence)

  1. Register a restaurant (creates the manager account)
  2. Manager sets up menu items and tables
  3. Register a customer account (in a different browser)
  4. Customer browses, adds to cart, and pays
  5. Manager updates the order status
  6. Customer checks that the order status changed
  7. Test table booking, reviews, profile settings, and the contact page

PART A — Restaurant manager testing

1 Open the registration page

  1. Open your browser and go to https://www.cuisineease.com
  2. Click Register (top of the page), or go directly to:
    https://www.cuisineease.com/auth/register
  3. On the welcome screen, click the Restaurant Admin option (the one with the shop icon)
✓ You should see: A page titled “Register Your Restaurant” showing “Step 1 of 3”.

2 Step 1 of 3 — Restaurant details

Fill in every required field, then click Next.

FieldExample to type
Restaurant nameTest Kitchen Buea
DescriptionA friendly test restaurant for CuisineEase testing
Restaurant emailbistro.test@example.com
Restaurant phone+237 677 111 001
Restaurant typePick one from the list (e.g. Restaurant)
Cuisine typesTick at least one (e.g. Cameroonian)

3 Step 2 of 3 — Location & opening hours

  1. Fill in Street, City (e.g. Buea), and Country (Cameroon)
  2. Latitude and longitude may fill in automatically — if not, use 4.15 and 9.24
  3. Under Booking days, tick at least Monday through Saturday
  4. Set Opening hours (e.g. open 08:00, close 22:00)
  5. Tick This restaurant offers delivery if you plan to test delivery orders
  6. Click Next

4 Step 3 of 3 — Manager account

FieldExample to type

Logo and cover photo are optional. Click Register restaurant.

✓ You should see: A “Check your email” page telling you to open the email sent to your manager address.

5 Set your manager password from email

  1. Open the email inbox for the manager email you used
  2. Look for an email from CuisineEase (check spam if needed)
  3. Click the link in the email to set your password
  4. Choose a password that follows the rules above (e.g. Manager1!)
If you do not receive the email within a few minutes, go to https://www.cuisineease.com/auth/forgot-password and request a new link.

6 Log in as manager

  1. Go to https://www.cuisineease.com/auth/login
  2. Enter your manager email and password
  3. Click Sign in / Login
After login, one of two things will happen:
  • Application under review — Your restaurant is waiting for CuisineEase team approval (can take up to 48 hours). This is normal for new restaurants. Contact the CuisineEase team if you need the account activated for testing.
  • Manager dashboard — You go straight to the manager area. Continue to Step 7.

7 Add a food item to the menu

  1. In the left menu, click Menu
  2. Click the Add New Menu button (top right)
  3. Fill in the form:
    • Name — e.g. Grilled Chicken
    • Category — pick one (e.g. Main Course)
    • Price — e.g. 3500 (in XAF)
    • Description — short description of the dish
    • Image — upload any food photo
    • Leave other fields as default if unsure
  4. Click Save
✓ You should see: The new dish appears in the menu list. Try clicking Edit and Delete on a test item to confirm those work too.

8 Add a table (for reservations)

  1. In the left menu, click Tables
  2. First, create a section (e.g. name it A — use a capital letter)
  3. Then click Add table
  4. Choose the section, set seats to 4, and save
✓ You should see: Your new table listed on the Tables page.

PART B — Customer testing

Use a private/incognito window or a different browser so you are not still logged in as the manager.

10 Register a customer account

  1. Go to https://www.cuisineease.com/auth/register
  2. Click Customer (the person icon)
  3. Fill in:
    • Full name — e.g. Test Customer
    • Email — e.g. customer.test@example.com
    • Phone — e.g. +237 677 222 003
    • Password — e.g. Customer1!
    • Confirm password — same as above
  4. Tick the box to accept Terms and Privacy Policy
  5. Click Register
✓ You should see: A message asking you to verify your email, and a page to enter a 6-digit code.

11 Verify your email

  1. Check your email inbox (and spam) for a verification code from CuisineEase
  2. Enter the 6-digit code on the verification page
  3. Click to confirm
  4. When successful, click Continue to Login

12 Log in as customer

  1. Go to https://www.cuisineease.com/auth/customer/login or /auth/login
  2. Enter your customer email and password
  3. Click Sign in
✓ You should see: The customer dashboard with a menu and sidebar (Restaurants, Cart, Order History, etc.).

13 Browse restaurants and food

  1. In the sidebar, click Restaurants
  2. Find the restaurant you registered (e.g. Test Kitchen Buea) and click on it
  3. Browse the menu — you should see the food item the manager added
  4. Click on a dish to see its details (name, price, description)

14 Add food to your cart

  1. On a dish page, choose any add-ons if shown (optional)
  2. Add special instructions if you want (optional)
  3. Click Add to Cart
  4. Click the Cart icon or go to Cart in the sidebar
✓ You should see: Your item in the cart with the correct name, quantity, and price.

15 Add to favourites (optional)

  1. On a restaurant or dish page, click the heart icon
  2. Go to Favorites in the sidebar
  3. Confirm the item or restaurant appears there

16 Pay and place your order

  1. Open your Cart
  2. Change the quantity using + / − buttons if you want
  3. Choose order type: Delivery, Takeout, or Dine-in
  4. On the payment side, select Mobile Money (recommended for testing)
  5. Choose MTN or Orange and enter a phone number (e.g. +237 677 222 003)
  6. Check the total — delivery fee may be added for delivery orders
  7. Click the button to place your order / pay
Card and PayPal options may appear on screen but are not fully connected yet. Always use Mobile Money for testing payments.
✓ You should see: A success message and your cart becomes empty.

17 Check your order history

  1. Click Order History in the sidebar
  2. Find the order you just placed
  3. Click on it to see details (items, total, status)
✓ You should see: Your order listed with status New or Pending.

18 Book a table (reservation)

  1. Go to Restaurants and open your test restaurant
  2. Find the Tables or Book a table section
  3. Pick a date, time, and number of guests
  4. Complete the booking steps and pay if asked (Mobile Money)
  5. Go to Reservations in the sidebar to confirm it appears

19 Leave a review

  1. On a restaurant or dish page, find the Reviews tab
  2. Give a star rating and write a short comment
  3. Submit the review
✓ You should see: Your review appears on the page after submitting.

20 Update your profile

  1. Click Settings in the sidebar
  2. Try updating your name or profile photo
  3. Open Notifications settings and toggle an option
  4. Open Security and confirm the page loads

PART C — Manager handles the customer order

Switch back to your manager browser (Step 6 login).

21 Find and update the order

  1. Click Orders in the manager sidebar
  2. Find the customer's order in the list
  3. Click on the order to open details
  4. Update the status step by step:
    • Click Start preparing
    • Then Mark ready
    • Then Mark delivered
✓ You should see: The status badge changes after each click.

22 Customer checks updated status

  1. Switch to the customer browser
  2. Go to Order History and open the same order
  3. Refresh the page if needed
✓ You should see: The order status matches what the manager set (Preparing → Ready → Delivered).

23 Manager checks reservation and review

  1. As manager, open Reservations — the customer's booking should appear
  2. Open Reviews — the customer's review should appear

Full testing checklist

Tick each item when it works correctly:

  • Restaurant registration (all 3 steps) works
  • Manager receives email and can set password
  • Manager can log in
  • Manager can add, edit, and delete a menu item
  • Manager can add a table section and table
  • All manager sidebar pages open without errors
  • Customer registration works
  • Customer email verification (6-digit code) works
  • Customer can log in
  • Customer can browse restaurants and see menu items
  • Customer can add items to cart
  • Customer can pay with Mobile Money and place an order
  • Order appears in customer Order History
  • Order appears in manager Orders
  • Manager can update order status (Preparing → Ready → Delivered)
  • Customer sees updated order status
  • Table reservation works (customer + manager)
  • Review submission works (customer + manager)
  • Customer profile/settings can be updated
  • Contact form on public website works
  • Public pages (Home, Menu, About) load correctly
  • Logout works
  • Wrong password shows a clear error

How to report a problem

If something does not work, copy the template below, fill it in, and send it to the CuisineEase team (cuisineease@gmail.com):

WHAT I WAS TRYING TO DO: (e.g. "Place an order as a customer") STEP NUMBER FROM THIS GUIDE: (e.g. "Step 16") WHAT I DID — step by step: 1. 2. 3. WHAT I EXPECTED TO HAPPEN: WHAT ACTUALLY HAPPENED: MY ROLE: Customer / Restaurant Manager / Visitor EMAIL USED FOR TESTING: BROWSER: Chrome / Firefox / Edge DEVICE: Phone / Laptop / Tablet SCREENSHOT: [attach a photo of the screen]

Things that may not work yet (this is normal)

  • Google sign-in — may not be fully set up; use email registration instead
  • Card and PayPal payments — use Mobile Money (MTN or Orange) for testing
  • Chat / Messages — may show "coming soon"
  • Restaurant approval — new restaurants may wait up to 48 hours before the manager dashboard is active
  • Menu import button — may not do anything yet
  • Some charts and statistics — may show sample or empty data until real orders exist
