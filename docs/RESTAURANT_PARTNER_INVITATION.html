What Is CuisineEase?

CuisineEase is a cloud-based restaurant management platform available on any device — phone, tablet, or desktop computer. It connects your restaurant directly to customers through a beautifully designed mobile app while giving you a powerful manager dashboard to control every aspect of your operations. The platform is available in English and French, and is designed to work reliably even on modest internet connections.

📱 Customer Mobile App

Your restaurant is listed in the CuisineEase app where customers browse your menu, place orders, make reservations, and track their food in real time.

🖥️ Manager Dashboard

Complete control over menus, orders, tables, staff, inventory, and analytics — all from one screen, accessible anywhere.

🍳 Chef Kitchen Display

A live, digital kitchen display replaces paper tickets. Orders appear instantly, organized by priority and station — reducing errors and speeding up service.

🚴 Driver & Delivery Network

Built-in driver management, real-time delivery tracking, and dispatch — your own delivery system without third-party integrations.

📊 Analytics & Reporting

Track sales, popular items, peak hours, staff performance, and delivery stats. Make decisions backed by real data, not guesswork.

👥 Staff Management

Invite and manage waiters, chefs, and drivers. Assign roles, track shifts, and monitor performance — all in one place.
